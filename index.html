About LILIUM

Lilium Cosmetics stand for Heritage and tradition, trends for the taste of an era. Lilium is one of the largest manufacturing facilities of organic and herbal cosmetics products in India.

It all started at 2005 when two genus had a brilliant idea: a new body, skin and hair care formula inspired from the heart of nature. Officially founded in 2009 by Mr. Mrityunjay Choudhary and Mr. Mukesh Choudhary created a brand name that has reflected until today its near proximity to deep green forests and flowering valley. Lilium Cosmetic products are known for quality and tradition in what has now become the highly popular field of natural cosmetics. Our products have become a complete natural face, body and hair care, with a growing fan base stretching around all the states of India and are r&d and produced at our own production facilities and research labs in India.
